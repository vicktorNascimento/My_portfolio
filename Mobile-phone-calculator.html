Calculator application

Music player application. When the application starts, it will load showing the logo. Then it will show an interface to log in, the user: "admin" and password: "1234", otherwise it will show that the password or username is incorrect. Once you have logged in, you will be able to choose the genre, then the artist, which will show an image of the artist. Depending on the artist, their songs will be shown, which when chosen will play the song. The album cover of the song, the artist's name, the song name, the progress bar showing how much of the song is left, and a pause image that you can pause the song and change to a play image to resume the song will be displayed on the bar. The interface of the app will also show a logout button in case you want to change user.
