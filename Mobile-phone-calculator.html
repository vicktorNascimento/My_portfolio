Calculator application

Mobile phone calculator. Its operation is through buttons and a text view which takes the text from the buttons and adds them up and when operating, it transforms them into numeric. You can operate with integers, decimals, negatives and also operate with all the operators available. You can also delete all the text or the last thing you entered. Finally, after each operation, another text will be displayed in another text view where you can see the last operation with its result. This way, you can continue to operate and have that information.
